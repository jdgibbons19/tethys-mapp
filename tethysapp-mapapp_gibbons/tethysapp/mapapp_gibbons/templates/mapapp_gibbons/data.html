Data Services

This map app has two data services. The following are the data services that were used and descriptions of each:

  • UDOT Annual Average Daily Traffic (AADT) Data
    • Source: UDOT Open Data Portal (Link)
    • Description: Contains average daily traffic volumes for state routes and federal aid routes in Utah.
    • Hosting Location: geoserver2.byu.edu
  • Elevation DEM Data for Utah County
    • Source: Utah AGRC (Link)
    • Description: Contains elevation data for most of Utah County.
    • Hosting Location: geoserver2.byu.edu
